EYA · Quartier polygon labEXPERIMENTAL

Side-by-side comparison of three draft-polygon strategies for the 310 quartiers of Cotonou + Abomey-Calavi. None of these are authoritative. Read README.md for what each version means and known limitations.

Polygon versions

Centroid-only nearest-seed partition. Ignores all topology.
Same as v1 but clipped to IGN arrondissement boundaries. Previous default.
Multi-source Dijkstra on 100 m grid. Water = forbidden · roads = cheap · POIs named after a quartier act as additional seeds.
v3 + per-quartier "home land component(s)". Stepping into a component NOT in the quartier's home set costs +8. Bridges (incl. Pont MLK) reconnect land masses. POI evidence in another component adds it to the home set.
v3 + per-quartier "home side" of each nearby canal/river/stream (>200 m). Stepping from home side into opposite side within 500 m of the polyline costs +25. POI evidence on the other bank flips that waterway to "both" (no penalty). Generic — not hardcoded.
v5 with a LOCAL fix to rasterize_water() applied only inside the bbox lat 6.370–6.400, lng 2.380–2.415. Inside the bbox: drain/ditch cells that share a 100 m raster cell with a road become claimable (the road wins). Outside the bbox: identical to v5. 33 cells freed; MIDÉDJI +11, TONATO +22, SETOVI +2, no losses.
Cells where 2nd-best owner's cost is close to best — fuzzy transition zones.
